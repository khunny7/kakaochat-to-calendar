Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 5, 2025

Privacy First: Kakao Chat to Calendar is designed with your privacy in mind. All file processing happens entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or chat files.

1. Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information.

Specifically:

  • We do NOT collect your chat files
  • We do NOT collect your messages or personal conversations
  • We do NOT collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do NOT use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do NOT collect usage analytics or statistics

2. How Your Data is Processed

When you use Kakao Chat to Calendar:

  • Local Processing Only: All file parsing and calendar generation happens entirely in your web browser
  • No Upload: Your KakaoTalk chat files never leave your device
  • No Storage: We do not store any files or data on our servers
  • Client-Side Only: The application runs entirely as client-side JavaScript in your browser

3. Data Storage

The application does not store any data. When you close the browser tab:

  • All processed data is deleted from browser memory
  • No data persists on our servers (because we have no servers)
  • You may optionally download the generated ICS calendar file to your device - this is stored locally under your control

4. Third-Party Services

This application is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect:

  • Basic server logs (IP address, browser type, access times)
  • This data is collected by GitHub, not by us
  • For GitHub's privacy policy, visit: GitHub Privacy Statement

5. Use of Generated Calendar Files

The ICS calendar files you download:

  • Are generated locally in your browser
  • Are stored on your device under your control
  • When imported to calendar services (Outlook, Google Calendar, etc.), those services' privacy policies apply
  • We have no access to or control over these calendar files once downloaded

6. Children's Privacy

Our service does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

8. Open Source

This application is open source. You can review the complete source code at:

https://github.com/khunny7/kakaochat-to-calendar

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through:

  • GitHub Issues: Report an Issue

10. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over your data at all times because it never leaves your device.
